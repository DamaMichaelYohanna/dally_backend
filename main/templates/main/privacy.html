Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Dally, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

What Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address, business name, and optional business details when you register.
  • Transaction Data: Sales, expenses, and other bookkeeping records you enter into the app.
  • Usage Data: How you use the app (e.g., which features you access) to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Dally service
  • Calculate tax estimates based on your transaction data
  • Send you important updates about the app
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., NDPR, FIRS requirements)

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely on servers within Nigeria or in jurisdictions with adequate data protection laws. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information.

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for:

  • Cloud hosting: To store your data securely
  • Email delivery: To send you account notifications
  • Analytics: To understand how people use Dally (anonymized data only)

These providers are required to protect your data and use it only as we direct.

Your Rights Under NDPR

As a Nigerian user, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a common format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email provided below.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we're required by law to retain it longer.

Children's Privacy

Dally is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by email or through the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@dally.ng
